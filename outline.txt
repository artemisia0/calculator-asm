Arithmetic expression calculator in asm that supports
operators (), +, - (binary, unary), *, /, % and floating-point numbers also.
It will work for double precision floating-point numbers only (fit in 8 bytes)

Operators priorities (from highest to lowest)
() (parentheses)
- (unary)
*, /, %
+, - (binary)

Example 1.
Input:
1 + (-1.1 + 2) * 2 % 1.5

Output:
1.3
